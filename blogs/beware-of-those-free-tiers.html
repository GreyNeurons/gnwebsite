Beware of those free tiers

Recently in a code review session for a product under development, goto know the team was using Firebase for in app notifications. Looked like a reasonable choice nevertheless reminded me of the infamous story how a startup almost went bankrupt using Firebase. I shared the story with the team & apply learnings if any from the same.

The story is really about how a startup got a bill of 72K USD overnight after launching their application. If you are interested you can read up the first part of the story here.

Techies should NOT skip the part 2 of the story where the author talks about the code in his application that led to this and how he fixed it.

When the story was published, it was discussed quite a bit on internet. Here is one from Y Combinator forum.

So next time you are building your app & want to use a cloud service, remember this blog & think hard if you really want to use yet another 'pay as you go' cloud service in your app.
